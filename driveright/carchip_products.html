CarChip®

All-in-one Driving and Engine Performance Monitor

Easy as 1-2-3!
No tools or automotive expertise required! Just plug CarChip into the On-board diagnostics (OBD) port (easily found under the dashboard or steering wheel) and it reads and stores data from your car's on-board computers, continuously logging driving and engine performance.

CarChip is perfect for:

  • Businesses with 1 or more vehicles
  • Auto enthusiasts
  • Hobbyists
  • Parents of teens
  • All Drivers who want to be able to reset a check-engine light or prove their speed if in an accident
  • Choose CarChip Connect if you own a business with vehicles to determine driving parameters - speed, acceleration, deceleration or location in real time. Knowing when and how vehicles are being used allows you to better allocate resources. CarChip Connect monitors emission status and engine diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs) and will alert you in real time when the engine light or other diagnostic indicator goes of.
  • Choose CarChip Pro if you have a small business or if you're someone who wants to monitor your own car/driving. The version of CarChip Pro comes with the software CD to analyze data, and a USB cable for downloading the data.
  • Choose CarChip Fleet Pro if you own a business with vehicles to track. Similar to CarChip Pro, CarChip Fleet Pro allows you to use GPS tracking, Wireless data loading, and our Fleet Management Software to keep track of your vehicles and drivers.
