CarChip® Fleet Pro

Simple installation, loads of information.

With CarChip Fleet Pro, installation is as simple as "plug and go." Just plug the tiny data logger into the on-board diagnostics port and begin recording trip details immediately.

Compatible with our GPS System and wireless System.

Features and Specs:

  • Engine performance monitoring keeps your vehicles in top condition.
  • Diagnostic trouble codes, freeze-frame sensor readings, and the ability to clear the check engine light.
  • Emissions readiness reports to check the vehicle’s smog-control systems status.
  • Stores up to 1200 hours of driving data.
  • Requires Fleet Management Software, sold separately.

To monitor engine performance, use a CarChip Fleet Pro in each vehicle. To monitor driver performance, issue each driver his own CarChip and ID number. Drivers can quickly and easily take their CarChip with them from one vehicle to the next. See our comparison charts for CarChip Fleet Pro and DriveRight.
