     Asbestos is the one responsible for the number of side effects, which can cause anything from discomfort to death.  Asbestoris and pleural plaques can result from exposure to asbestos and more notably so can mesothelioma, a deadly cancer that can take many decades to develop but only a matter of months from the onset of symptoms to kill.

     Mesothelioma is a deadly cancerous disease that is becoming more and more common.  It affects the mesothelial cells that make up the mesothelium-the outer lining protecting the body's major organs such as heart, stomach and lungs.

     Malignant mesothelioma is a fatal disease cause by exposure to asbestos.  Mesothelioma does not manifest itself following asbestos exposure-the average manifestation time is 35-40 years and in some cases can reach up to 50 years or later.

     As a result of asbestos exposure, mesothelioma comes in three forms: pleural mesothelioma, peritoneal mesothelioma, and pericardial mesothelioma.  All three types have a variety of symptoms.
